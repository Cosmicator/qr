Frequently Asked Questions

Are these generated QR codes free to use?
Yes! All QR codes generated on this platform are completely free for both personal and commercial use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or watermarks.
Do these QR codes ever expire?
No. We generate static QR codes, which means your text or URL is encoded directly into the image itself. Because it doesn't rely on our servers to redirect, they will never expire and will work forever.
Which file format should I download?
PNG and JPG formats are great for web use, emails, and social media. If you are printing your QR code on a business card, poster, or billboard, we highly recommend downloading the SVG or EPS vector formats so they can be scaled infinitely without losing quality.
Can I track how many people scan my QR code?
Static QR codes do not have built-in tracking capabilities. If you need scan analytics, you can use a third-party link shortener (like Bitly) as your destination URL, and generate the QR code using that shortened link.
Why won't my custom QR code scan?
The most common reason a code won't scan is a lack of contrast. Make sure your background color is significantly lighter than your dot colors. Additionally, verify that your uploaded center logo isn't so large that it covers up critical data patterns in the code.
